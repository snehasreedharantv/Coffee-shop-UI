About Touché

Crafting experiences, one cup at a time.

Our Story

Touché Café was born out of a passion for fine coffee, delicious food, and authentic connections. Located in the heart of Los Angeles, we blend European elegance with local charm to create a café experience like no other.

Whether you're sipping espresso with friends, enjoying a quiet brunch, or grabbing a to-go latte, Touché is your cozy retreat. Our team takes pride in delivering exceptional service and unforgettable flavors in every cup and plate.

Our Philosophy

At Touché, we believe that every visit should feel special. We focus on quality, hospitality, and community.

Fresh Ingredients

We source the best beans and ingredients to ensure every bite and sip is pure perfection.

Warm Ambience

Our space is designed to make you feel at home — elegant, comfortable, and always welcoming.

People First

Touché isn’t just a café. It’s a community space where smiles are shared and stories begin.
